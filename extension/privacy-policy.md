# IPA Reader - Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

## Data Collection
IPA Reader does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All processing is performed locally in your browser.

## Permissions
- **Storage**: Used to save your preferences locally
- **TTS**: Used for text-to-speech functionality
- **Scripting**: Used to inject content scripts into web pages
- **Context Menus**: Used to add "Speak Selection" to the right-click menu

## Contact
If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
